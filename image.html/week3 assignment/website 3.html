Clayquest by Mitchy

Clayquest by Mitchy was started by Moizzah Afzal in 2020 amid Covid. Due to an increasing rise in online businesses, this jewelry brand rose to fame in less than a year.

About Clayquest

Clayquest is a quest to finding and making the best quality polymer clay jewelry for the consumer. Our consumers love the uncrackable jewelry that offers them a unique and stylish look. Apart from that, we also create beautiful handmade rings and necklaces embellished with fine quality pearls and beads.
